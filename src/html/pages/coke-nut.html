coke nut

Coke nut is used as a fuel and carbon reducing agent in ferroalloy and electrode production, in non-ferrous metallurgy and other industries

It is characterized by a long burning time and high calorific value. Due to such properties, coke nut is actively used in the production of ferroalloys.
